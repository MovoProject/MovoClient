Frequently asked questions

How to contact MOVO group?

In order to receive customer support or send users feedbacks to us, please contact us at our email support@movo.uk. Related to the developers issues, please contact Wallet.devs@movo.uk

Why do swap exchange rate changes?

When choosing a Swap order amount, you may notice that the exchange rate changes frequently. This is because cryptocurrency markets are always open and their values are constantly changing.

To reflect the latest market price, our exchange rates update in real time and will vary. This means that your rate will change depending on when your Swap transaction occurs and what the market price is at that exact time.

With our real-time updates, you can rest assured that Swap orders placed within your Movo Blockchain Wallet will be executed at the current exchange rate at the time of your transaction.

Do I need to set up two-factor authentication?

Yes, you need to set up two-factor authentication through SMS code and Google authenticator to protect your wallet from unauthorized access.

What should I do when losing my password?

Our wallet is not monitored, we do not back up your password, your log in code is sent to you via SMS or log in code via your email.

What does "SWAP" mean?

Swap is Blockchain’s crypto-to-crypto exchange. Swap allows users to quickly and easily exchange one crypto asset for another, without having to use fiat currency as an intermediary.

Fully integrated within our non-custodial wallet, Swap users can exchange assets on-chain

What crypto assets can I Swap?

Swap currently allows users to exchange between four different cryptocurrencies: bitcoin, ethereum, movo, bitcoin cash, and USDT. This list will continue to grow as we introduce support for new crypto assets within the Movo Blockchain wallet.

What fees are applied to my Cryptocurrency swap transactions?

Movo applies fees for each swap transaction including fixed fee and variable fee based on the total amount of money and payment method or the amount deposited. For smaller buying and selling transactions, the application shall apply a fixed fee. The fees for small transactions as below:

- 0,99 USD to swap an amount from 11,99 USD and downwards

- 1,49 USD to swap an amount from 12,00 USD to 27,49 USD

- 1,99 USD to swap an amount from 27,50 USD to 52,99 USD

-2,99 USD swap an amount from 53,00 USD to 200 USD

- For swap transaction from $200.05 and onwards, the fees will be subjected to an amount of 1,68% increment.
